◘ How to Play

Each scene presents a rich, detailed image filled with hidden objects. Your goal is to find all 10 objects before time runs out. Tap on an object when you spot it — the faster you find them, the more stars you earn.

Stuck on a tricky object? Use the built-in hint system to reveal its location. Progress through all 20 levels and see if you can achieve a perfect three-star rating on every scene.
